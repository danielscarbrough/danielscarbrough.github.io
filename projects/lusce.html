LUSCE Junior Project

In my junior year at university, I took the initiative to participate as a junior member in a senior design team. I ended up joining the LUSCE senior design team, which stands for LeTourneau University Smart Charging Exploration. The team was tasked with developing a charging station for a small 72V electric vehicle, powered by a mobile solar panel station. Junior members are tasked with assisting the team for three hours a week, but I ended up working an average of six or seven hours weekly. While I entered the project with an electrical engineering mindset, the team was lacking experience in mechanical engineering. I volunteered to assist the sole mechanical engineering senior on the team in developing a chassis for our electronic components, and a stand for our solar panels.
